Fire Seasons in Australia

Bushfire seasons vary across Australian states.

November 1, 2015 Jane Doe

Season variations are due to climate zones, rainfall patterns and vegetation types that determine when fuels (grass, scrub, forests) dry out enough to burn.
State led fire authorities determine the state bushfire season each year.

Australian fire seasons vary by state and region due to climate differences, southern states peak in summer (Dec-Feb) and northern/tropical areas earlier (May-Oct).
