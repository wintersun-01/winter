    Bushfire seasons vary across Australian states.

    November 1, 2015 Jane Doe
    • Bushfire season variations across states are due to climate zones, rainfall patterns and when vegetation (grass, scrub and forests) dry out enough to burn.
    • Fire authorities in each state determine the bushfire season each year for that state.
    • Select states to view bushfire seasons.
    • Southern states peak in summer (Dec-Feb)
    • Northern and tropical areas peak earlier. (May-Oct).
